Why add your voice?

Conflict in Yemen has triggered the world's most acute humanitarian crisis. Children are dying from hunger every day. A whole generation of Yemeni children are at risk of losing their future. With nearly two million children suffering from severe malnutrition, the time has come to raise our voices.

WFP is on the front lines, providing more than seven million Yemenis with critical support. We stand ready to feed 14 million people, but we cannot afford any further disruption to our life-saving operations. Getting food into Yemen is essential.

We need your voice to help us call upon those waging war to allow the free flow of humanitarian food into the country and safety for aid workers feeding children on the brink of starvation.
